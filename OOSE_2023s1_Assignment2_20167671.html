1   Your   Task

Demonstrate   the  correct usage  of OOSE  concepts   by   designing   and   implementing  a simple  software application,  in   accordance   with   the   theme  described below. ̢̡̧̧̡̠̦̦

3   Problem   Theme

This   assignment   does  not have  specific technical  requirements,   other   than   as   mentioned  above. However,  there is  a   theme   that   your   application  must follow,  along with  a   set   of   technical   suggestions. ̢̡̧̧̡̠̦̦

The  theme is  public transport  ticketing,   in   which   we   care  about (for  instance) keeping  track   of   the   following: ̢̡̧̧̡̠̦̦

  • Passengers’   accounts  and identities; ̢̡̧̧̡̠̦̦
  • Whether  (for instance)  passengers’   accounts   are   in   good  standing, in  debt, or  cancelled; ̢̡̧̧̡̠̦̦
  • Whether   a   passenger   is   currently  onboard a  particular bus/train  or   not; ̢̡̧̧̡̠̦̦
  • The   exact   times   passengers  swipe their  credit card  (or   other   card)   to   enter/leave  a bus  or train; ̢̡̧̧̡̠̦̦
  • Whether  certain   busses   and   trains   are  on-time, delayed,  or cancelled; ̢̡̧̧̡̠̦̦
  • The  physical   location   of   busses   and  trains within  the transport  network. ̢̡̧̧̡̠̦̦

We   can’t   create   the   “real  thing” exactly,  but consider  how   we   might   simulate   these  things. You  can read  data   from   a   file   and/or  the console  (to represent  information   about   the   transport   system,  including events  that take  place).   You   can   write   information  to a  file and/or  the   console   to   indicate   what  happens during  the simulation.  The   exact   input   and   output  formats are  up to  you. ̢̡̧̧̡̠̦̦

Note ̢̡̧̧̡̠̦̦

Yes,   this   section   is   intentionally  vague. This  may not  be   the   kind   of   software  development assignment  you’re used  to. ̢̡̧̧̡̠̦̦

Your   system   must   be   a  single, coherent,  working software  application,   but   you   must   decide  on the  exact technical  requirements   for   yourself.   You   need  not implement  everything mentioned  in   this   section.   You   may  implement things  that are  not   specifically   mentioned,   if   you  wish, provided  they follow  the   general   theme. ̢̡̧̧̡̠̦̦

Consider   the   functionality  of your  application as  simply   a   means   to   demonstrate  your OOSE  knowledge. You  only   need   to   implement   just  enough functionality  to help  fulfill   the   marking   criteria   listed  at the  top. Don’t  feel   that   you   need   to  do any  more than  this. ̢̡̧̧̡̠̦̦
